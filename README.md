Are you tired of missing out on unique airplanes flying over your house? Me too! After missing out on N757AF, I've decided to rebuild Flyover Radar to take advantage of OpenSky's 25sq° bounding box. Now with a 100 mile radius instead of the previous' 6, you will recieve alerts aircraft that have a chance of flying over your house. You should have much more time to "react".
